The Print Plus Widget will create a print ready document in one of the following formats. The document will appear in a new tab or a new window, depending on your browser and its settings.
  • PDF
  • JPG
  • PNG8
  • PNG32
  • GIF
  • EPS
  • SVG
  • SVGZ
The print ready document will be formatted and scaled for one of the following layouts:
  • Letter Landscape (ANSI A)
  • Letter Portrait (ANSI A)
  • Tabloid Landscape (ANSI B)
  • Tabloid Portrait (ANSI B)
  • A4 Landscape
  • A4 Portrait
  • A3 Landscape
  • A3 Portrait
  • Just the Map



The map area and paper outline will be shown in red over the map. The paper outline can be moved to the area of interest by clicking and dragging it with the mouse.

The print scale can be adjusted by entering the desired scale or by using the scale slider control. As the print scale changes, the size of the paper outline will change.

If a paper size is selected and the Use Title Block checkbox is selected, the Title and Author for the title block can be entered.

When the layout template is set to Just the Map the map will be exported with no title block or borders. There will be a diagram on the Settings dropdown showing the portion of the map visible in the browser and the portion of the map to be exported. The portion of the map to be exported can be changed by adjusting the scale of the map, changing the Preserve setting, or changing the Printed Map Dimensions.
